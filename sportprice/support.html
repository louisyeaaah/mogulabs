Troubleshooting

AI scan is not recognizing my food

For best results:

  1. Make sure the food is clearly visible and well-lit.
  2. Avoid scanning multiple different dishes in one photo.
  3. Hold your phone steady and wait for the camera to focus.
  4. Try scanning from a slightly different angle if the first attempt fails.

HealthKit data is not syncing

  1. Open iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.
  2. Tap SportPrice and make sure all relevant permissions are enabled.
  3. If permissions were denied previously, toggle them off and back on.
  4. Restart the app and check again.

Subscription not showing as active

  1. Make sure you are signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.
  2. Open SportPrice and go to Settings > Restore Purchases.
  3. Check iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions to verify the subscription status.
  4. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or your device.

App is crashing or freezing

  1. Make sure you are running the latest version of SportPrice from the App Store.
  2. Ensure your device is running iOS 17.0 or later.
  3. Force quit the app (swipe up from the app switcher) and reopen it.
  4. If the problem continues, try restarting your device.
  5. Still having trouble? Email us with a description of what you were doing when it happened.
