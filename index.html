Vela - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Vela ("we", "our", or "the app") is a health and fitness app designed to help you track your wellness journey. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Data Collection and Storage

Vela stores all your health and fitness data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal health data on any external servers.

  • Health Data: Vela reads health data from Apple HealthKit (such as activity, sleep, heart rate, and workout data) with your explicit permission. This data is processed and stored entirely on your device.
  • AI Coaching: AI-powered coaching features process data locally on your device. Your health data is not sent to any cloud AI services.
  • No Account Required: Vela does not require you to create an account or provide any personal identifying information.

Apple HealthKit

Vela integrates with Apple HealthKit to read your health and fitness data. We adhere to Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • We only access HealthKit data with your explicit permission.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties.
  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through your device's Settings.

In-App Purchases

Vela offers optional subscription plans and in-app purchases. All transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Vela may use the following third-party services:

  • Sentry: For crash reporting and app stability monitoring. Crash reports may include device type and OS version, but do not contain health data or personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your health and fitness data remains on your device at all times.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed. We do not retain any copies of your data.

Children's Privacy

Vela is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
